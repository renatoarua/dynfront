Static Line The static line is a graphic tool to analyze displacements due to static forces applied to system (distributed mass of the rotor). The user can find 4 important information in this graphic: translational displacements of the center of shaft cross sections, angular/rotational displacements of shaft cross sections, force and moment reactions in bearings, and bearings positions.
